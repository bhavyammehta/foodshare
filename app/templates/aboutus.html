About FoodShare

Who We Are

FoodShare is a social initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger by connecting surplus food to people who need it most. We believe no food should be wasted when someone is hungry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a sustainable food-sharing ecosystem where excess food from restaurants, events, and households reaches the needy safely and efficiently.

We leverage technology to connect donors with NGOs and volunteers, making food donation simple, fast, and impactful.

Our Vision

To create a world where hunger is eliminated and food resources are used responsibly for the benefit of society and the environment.
